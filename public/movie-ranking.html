Bob's Favorite Movies

My Top Three Movies of All Time



The Big Lebowski

Quirky stoner flick about an aging hippie who has to solve a crime for a disabled millionaire with the same name while dodging nihilists, pornographers, the chief of the Malibu police department, and other typical Los Angeles inhabitants.

Apocalypse Now

Based on Joseph Conrad's Heart of Darkness but set in Vietnam instead of the Congo, with commentary on American foreign policy and pressure-driven insanity.

The Score

Robert DeNiro meets Edward Norton in a heist film based in Montreal. DeNiro is hired by his "agent" and friend (played by Marlon Brando) to pull off a robbery with an inside man.
